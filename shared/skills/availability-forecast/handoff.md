# Handoff

## The Skill Must Return

- the `adUnitIds` or the `lineItemId` used;
- the delivery window;
- the `creativeSizes` and targeting assumptions;
- the key forecast numbers;
- an actionable business recommendation.

## Open Risks to Surface

- incomplete or approximate targeting IDs;
- missing creative sizes in a display scenario;
- forecast insufficient compared with the objective;
- assumptions still too broad for a final go decision.

## Next Logical Step

- adjust inventory or targeting if the forecast is too low;
- otherwise continue to trafficking, preview, or deployment.
