# Handoff

## The Skill Must Return

- the resource IDs used: `jobId`, `orderId`, `lineItemIds`, `creativeIds`;
- the useful preview URLs;
- the coverage status;
- the campaign action result or the associated preview;
- the report result IDs or links;
- the post-action audit summary.

## Open Risks to Surface

- incomplete creative coverage;
- previews not yet validated by humans;
- campaign action still waiting for confirmation;
- budget or underdelivery alerts detected;
- need for update, pause, or rollback after analysis.

## Next Logical Step

- move to optimization if the campaign is delivering correctly;
- otherwise continue to `update_campaign`, `pause_campaign`, `archive_campaign`, or deeper reporting analysis.
