- confirm the format family: audio, video, or mixed companions;
- validate targeting, placements, dates, and delivery windows before creative creation;
- attach any forecast summary when supply or pacing is uncertain.

- create the correct creative type from the approved media file;
- associate the creative only after business review of names, landing rules, and destinations;
- surface any manual companion or packaging dependency instead of hiding it.

- review preview URLs and coverage before activation;
- document which checks were automated and which stayed manual;
- hand off creative IDs, line-item IDs, delivery context, and residual risks to deployment.
